Why Learn Semantic HTML?

Semantic HTML gives meaning to your content. Tags like <header>, <nav>, and <article> make your code more readable and SEO-friendly.

Accessibility Benefits

Screen readers use semantic tags to navigate a webpage, helping visually impaired users understand the layout and content easily.
